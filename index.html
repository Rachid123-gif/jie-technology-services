Who we are

Where expertise meets innovation

Ji&E Technology Services is a private company based in Rabat, Morocco, jointly owned and managed by Jilya Holding and Eltel Technologistics Ltd.

We develop and supply a wide range of unique technologies and logistical solutions for the institutional market in general, and for the defence & security markets in particular.
