Sector by sector

Challenges, solutions and the value we create

Defence

Mission-critical systems, integration, simulators and through-life support for armed forces — backed by communications and cryptography certification.

Challenges

Defence forces operate complex, multi-vendor systems that must remain ready and secure under the harshest operational conditions.

Solutions

We deliver systems integration, advanced simulators, shooting ranges and training centres, with certified through-life support up to Level D.

Benefits

Higher mission readiness, lower lifecycle cost and a single accountable partner from design to sustainment.

Aerospace

Aircraft and spacecraft engineering, MRO and supply of avionics, spare parts and landing gear, delivered by skilled technical manpower.

Challenges

Ageing fleets, demanding airworthiness standards and tight aircraft availability windows leave no room for error.

Solutions

Aerospace engineering, maintenance, repair and overhaul, plus sourcing of avionics, landing gear and certified spare parts.

Benefits

Maximised airworthiness, faster turnaround and dependable parts availability across the fleet lifecycle.

Aviation

A complete catalogue of airport ground support equipment and aircraft handling, backed by full technical services.

Challenges

Airports demand rapid, safe turnarounds with ground fleets that stay available across intense daily cycles.

Solutions

Supply and maintenance of GPUs, ACUs, tugs, jacks and baggage handlers, with on-site technical support.

Benefits

Smoother apron operations, reduced aircraft delays and higher equipment uptime.

Homeland Security

Screening, inspection and access-control systems for borders, customs and critical infrastructure, with certified maintenance.

Challenges

Borders and sensitive sites must detect evolving threats without slowing legitimate flows of people and goods.

Solutions

HLS screening and inspection systems, access control and customs applications, with certified installation and upkeep.

Benefits

Stronger threat detection, faster throughput and assured compliance at every checkpoint.

Transportation

Fleet and vehicle maintenance engineered for extreme environments, with advanced diagnostics and rapid repair.

Challenges

Vehicle fleets in demanding terrain and climates face heavy wear and costly unplanned downtime.

Solutions

Preventive and corrective maintenance, computerised diagnostics and rapid repair tailored to harsh operating conditions.

Benefits

Extended vehicle life, higher availability and predictable maintenance budgets.

Railway

Carriage assembly, system integration, retrofitting, component maintenance and professional painting.

Challenges

Rolling stock must be modernised and kept in service safely while minimising time out of operation.

Solutions

Carriage assembly, system integration, retrofitting, component maintenance and industrial painting.

Benefits

Modernised fleets, extended service life and reliable, safe operation.

Government Institutions

Secure software and hardware, system integration and test facilities, built to institutional compliance requirements.

Challenges

Public institutions need secure, interoperable systems that meet strict regulatory and confidentiality standards.

Solutions

Secure software and hardware development, system integration and built-to-spec test facilities, leveraging communications and cryptography certification.

Healthcare

Maintenance of medical imaging, emergency-care and training equipment, plus conversion of security technology into certified civilian systems.

Solutions

Maintenance of medical imaging, emergency-care and training systems, and certified conversion of security technologies for medical use.
