About Us

Engineering trust at the heart of Morocco's technology future

A Moroccan technology company delivering advanced engineering, defence and aerospace solutions across Africa and international markets.

Who we are

A trusted Moroccan technology partner

Ji&E Technology Services is a private company based in Rabat, Morocco, jointly owned and managed by Jilya Holding and Eltel Technologistics Ltd.

We develop and supply a wide range of unique technologies and logistical solutions for the institutional market in general, and for the defence and security markets in particular.

Backed by more than 20 years of combined expertise, we design, integrate, test and maintain advanced systems for the most demanding operational environments.
