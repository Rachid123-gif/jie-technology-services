02 · Engineering

Engineering & Design Services

Engineering design for aviation, development of ATE testing facilities, mechanical design and software. We provide built-to-spec solutions based on customer requirements and accepted development standards, including the execution of surveys and risk management.

Area A

Development & Integration of Security Systems

We develop unique software and hardware for security bodies, including interfaces to training systems. Over the years we have gained extensive experience in the development, integration and conversion of security systems into standardised civilian and medical systems.

Area B

Development & Production of Test Facilities

We develop and manufacture ATE (Automatic Test Equipment) on a generic, flexible platform that runs functional tests and supports Proof-of-Design (PoD) processes, integration and production — across electro-optics, electronics, software, firmware, communications, RF, propulsion, robotics and power supplies.
